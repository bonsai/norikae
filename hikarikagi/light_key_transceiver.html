4-Bit Protocol

  • Each character = 1 byte (8 bits)
  • Transmitted as 2 × 4-bit nibbles
  • First: HIGH nibble (bits 7-4)
  • Second: LOW nibble (bits 3-0)
  • Default: "HELLO WORLD!" at 0.5s/char
